# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in DrawShare a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Standards

Positive behaviour includes:
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Focusing on the problem, not the person
- Accepting that different people have different opinions and priorities

Unacceptable behaviour includes:
- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any behaviour that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately. The maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
